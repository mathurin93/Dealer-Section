How Does Bird Divert™ UV Work ?

Did you know that most birds can see UV light, while humans can’t?

Bird Divert uses ultraviolet (UV) reflective technology that takes advantage of most birds' ability to see in the UV spectrum, a capability humans lack. The markers are designed to reflect UV wavelengths up to 420 nanometers, creating a pattern birds recognize as a barrier, while remaining practically undetectable to the human eye.
